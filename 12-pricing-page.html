Common questions.

What counts as a "verification"?

One verification = one COA (certificate of analysis) uploaded and verified. Re-verifying the same COA doesn't count twice. Verifying 10 batches of the same product counts as 10 verifications.

Can I change plans?

Yes. Upgrade any time, downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. No contracts (except Enterprise).

What industries do you serve?

Cannabis, CBD, supplements, kratom, mushroom, peptides, alt-wellness, luxury, collectibles, cosmetics, specialty food & beverage. If you have a COA and you're regulated or high-risk, BrandrAI works for you.

How does payment partnership work?

We partner with 5+ high-risk payment processors through our Payments & Partnerships advisor. BrandrAI doesn't process payments directly — we route you to the best-fit partner for your vertical and volume. No PCI scope on your end.

What's the Partner Program?

For consulting firms, agencies, and law firms who want to offer BrandrAI to their clients. Includes a reseller dashboard, white-label branding, and revenue share. Learn more
