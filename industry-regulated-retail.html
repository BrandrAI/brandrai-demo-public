The problem for licensed operators

Your customer can't see your state compliance data.

You pay for lab tests. You file results with the state. That data proves your products are safe. But at retail, a customer scans a QR code on your packaging and gets… what exactly?

Consumer blindness

Your customer can't access state compliance data. They see a PDF a brand uploaded, which could be edited, substituted, or forged.

Counterfeit packaging

Knock-off brands copy your packaging and lab test PDFs without authorization. Consumers can't tell which is real.

Multi-state fragmentation

Each state runs its own compliance infrastructure. If you're licensed in 3 states, your brand lives in 3 disconnected silos.

Brand collision

Thousands of licensed brands with similar names. No federal trademark for many categories. Confusion at the shelf.

How it works

Your state data becomes your brand proof.

BrandrAI connects to your state compliance system, ingests your compliance data in real time, and surfaces it to consumers via branded verify pages. Three steps.

STEP 1

Connect

Link your state compliance system credentials once. Read-only API access to your own compliance data. Your credentials never leave your account.

  • State compliance system live pull
  • Multi-state unification
  • Per-license key management
STEP 2

Sign

Every batch gets a SHA-256 cryptographic signature bound to your compliance data + lab results. Tamper-evident. Counterfeit-resistant.

  • Cryptographic batch-level signatures
  • Optional on-chain anchoring
  • Cross-state verifiability
STEP 3

Verify

Generate branded QR codes. Consumers scan, see a public verify page with live compliance data, pass/fail status, and batch history. No login required.

  • Branded verify pages at your domain
  • Mobile-first QR scan flow
  • Re-verification — consumers validate hash locally
