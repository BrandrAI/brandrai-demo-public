Why now

Brand asset management just entered its fourth era.

Each era solved a real problem. Each one became table stakes. The fourth one is starting now — and the brands that move first own the next decade.

2005–2015

Era 1 · File storage

Most brands stored assets in Google Drive or Dropbox — and still do. Enterprise DAMs (Bynder, Brandfolder, Frontify) launched for big budgets but never reached most operators.

2015–2022

Era 2 · Guidelines

Pro DAMs added brand guidelines for the marketing teams that could afford them. Most brands still couldn’t justify the cost.

2022–2025

Era 3 · AI broke trust

Midjourney and ChatGPT made faking any brand trivial. Customers stopped trusting what they see.

2026 →

Era 4 · Verification

When fakes are free, proof is the moat. BrandrAI is the brand asset platform built for this era.
