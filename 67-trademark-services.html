Common questions.

Can cannabis brands actually get federal trademarks?

Not for products directly containing THC — USPTO refuses them because cannabis is federally Schedule I. But you CAN federally register for hemp-derived products (<0.3% THC), CBD where permitted, cannabis-adjacent apparel, smoke accessories, and "lifestyle" brands. Every legal state allows state-level trademark registration for in-state cannabis marks. Our Tier 3 covers state filings; Tier 4 covers USPTO for federally-eligible marks.

Is BrandrAI a law firm?

No. We're a tech platform. Actual legal work — filings, oppositions, enforcement — is done by licensed trademark attorneys in our '68 Partners network. You sign directly with the attorney and pay transparent fees. BrandrAI handles the tech, tracking, and monitoring layer.

What happens if someone else already has my name?

The Conflict Scan surfaces it before you spend money on a doomed filing. If there's a conflict, we recommend three paths: (1) differentiate the name, (2) negotiate coexistence with the existing mark holder, or (3) lean harder on cryptographic verification to establish consumer-visible proof even without exclusive rights to the name. Your BrandrAI verify page works regardless of trademark status.

Why pair trademark with cryptographic verification?

Trademarks give you the legal right to stop infringers — but enforcement takes months and money. Cryptographic on-pack verification stops confusion at the moment of purchase. Together: consumers can always tell it's really you (crypto), and if someone tries to copy you anyway, you have legal teeth (trademark). Layer 1 + Layer 2.

Which verticals do you cover?

Cannabis, CBD, hemp, kratom, supplements, peptides, mushroom, nootropics, specialty food & beverage, regulated cosmetics. If it's a regulated or high-risk category where counterfeits and knockoffs are hurting your business, we cover it.
