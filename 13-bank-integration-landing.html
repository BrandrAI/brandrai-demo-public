The problem

Sponsor banks can't verify high-risk merchants at scale.

Manual underwriting is slow.

Cannabis, CBD, supplement merchant applications take 3-7 days to review. Meanwhile, competitors onboard faster.

Fraud slips through.

Fake COAs. Counterfeit products. One bad merchant = chargebacks, fines, and association risk that affects your whole book.

Compliance data is siloed.

Lab results, brand registrations, license numbers — all in different systems. Nobody has a unified view.

The solution

A verified merchant directory with an API.

BrandrAI maintains tamper-proof verifications for every COA, every brand, every batch. Banks integrate our API and instantly know if a merchant is who they claim to be.

Real-time merchant lookup

Query by business name, EIN, or license number. Get verification status, license validity, COA history, and risk score in a single API call.

Webhook monitoring

Subscribe to changes on specific merchants. Get notified when their license lapses, COA is flagged, or verification status changes.

COA / lab test verification

Submit a merchant-provided COA PDF and get tamper-detection + cryptographic hash verification in 30 seconds. Integrates directly into your application flow.

Admin dashboard

Your team sees a unified view of all merchants using BrandrAI. Filter by risk score, verification status, vertical. Export audit-ready reports.

"Verified by BrandrAI" badge

Display BrandrAI verification status in your merchant-facing dashboards. Merchants see their trust level. Banks get the compliance story.

Enterprise security

SOC2 Type 2 in progress. SSO / SAML available. Data encryption at rest and in transit. No PCI scope passed to your systems.
