06 · The Trust Collapse
Start with why

No one trusts what they see online anymore.

And that changes every purchase decision in regulated commerce. This isn't a 2026 problem. It's been building for six years.

2019
Pre-COVID
~11% of retail online. Trust the store, the shelf, the review.
2020-22
COVID shift
+$1T e-commerce. Resale 5x faster than retail. DoorDash, StockX, Whatnot explode.
2023
TEMU + knockoff era
200M+ US TEMU users. ~40% of Amazon listings flagged as counterfeit. Reddit becomes #1 trust source.
2024
AI slop flood
GenAI goes mainstream. ~1,000 AI news sites. Fake reviews, fake photos, fake people. Deepfakes +3,000% YoY.
2026 — now
Default disbelief
71% of consumers can't tell AI from human content. If it's online, assume it's fake until proven real.

Reviews broke first

63% of consumers now trust Reddit + peers over brand claims. Amazon reviews — the gold standard in 2015 — are assumed bought or bot-generated.

Product photos next

Generated product images are indistinguishable from studio shots. TEMU uses them to clone real brands in 48 hours. Your packaging can be on a counterfeit SKU tonight.

Then founders

Deepfake CEO videos endorsing fake products. Consumers can't tell. The only thing left is cryptographic provable truth — on-pack, scannable, live.

When no one trusts anything they see, the brand that can prove it's real wins the decade.

No federal trademark

Cannabis is Schedule I — USPTO won't register the mark. State-level + common-law only. 2,000+ cannabis trademark disputes yearly, most unresolved.

BrandrAI: crypto trademark that works today

A cryptographic, scannable, tamper-evident identity bound to your brand + every batch. Trademark Services tier layers state registrations + USPTO filings (CBD/hemp/lifestyle) on top.

Cannabis is the wedge, not the ceiling. The brand-collision and counterfeit pattern is universal — supplements, peptides, kratom, mushrooms, vapes, CBD, skincare, luxury, collectibles — every vertical with real value to protect has it. We start in regulated commerce because regulated commerce pays first, then expand to every brand that can't afford to be fake. BrandrAI is the operating system for brands that have something real to prove.
