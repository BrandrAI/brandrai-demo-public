3 · Audience-specific follow-through

MSO Multi-state operators

Their pain: Metrc in one state, BioTrack in another, totally different SKU systems. When a batch fails testing in one state, pulling it cleanly across states is a nightmare. Consumers don't know which "Green Thumb" brand is the real one on a confusing shelf.

What Proof-of-brand layer gives them:

  • Single source of truth across all states — MA, RI, ME, NY, OH, MI, IL, etc.
  • Instant cross-state recall: one click flags a batch, verify pages switch to recall notice in real time
  • Consumer-visible QR on pack that proves authenticity at every shelf, every state
  • Unified brand asset management across their 40+ state-specific brands

Ask: “Would a 30-min call with Max make sense? He's running the Ohio launch event April 30. We'd love to show you how this would work on your portfolio.”

BRAND Regional / state brands

Their pain: consumer trust, knockoff confusion, no federal trademark option, expensive to do brand work themselves, paying three vendors for three overlapping functions (photography + COA hosting + web).

What BrandrAI gives them:

  • AI-generated on-brand product photography (stuff Meta / Google won't do for cannabis)
  • COA verification with tamper-evident QR on pack
  • WordPress/WooCommerce sync for their existing site
  • Trademark services layered on top — state registrations + USPTO for hemp / CBD
  • Free plan to try before paying

Ask: “Drop your card. I'll have Max follow up with a 15-min walkthrough and a free-plan invite. No commitment. If you come to Ohio May 1 as a visiting brand, we'll get you set up onsite.”

RETAIL Dispensaries

Their pain: brand intake process is slow (verifying lab results, checking license status, confirming what's in the jar). Budtender misunderstandings. Customer complaints when product doesn't match the label.

What BrandrAI does for them:

  • “BrandrAI Verified” badge on dispensary menus for brands that are on the platform (trust signal for consumers browsing online)
  • Faster intake: brand has a verified URL you can scan at receiving
  • Budtender tool: scan QR on pack to pull up full lab result + terpene profile

Ask: “Would your buyers want a partnership where verified brands get priority intake? Let me introduce you to Max after the show.”

PARTNER Ancillary services

Their angle: POS platforms, cannabis-specific payment processors, insurance brokers, compliance consultants, cannabis banks. Not customers. Partners.

The partnership pitch:

  • Their customers are our target customers. Reciprocal referral loop.
  • BrandrAI customers need banking, payments, POS — we route to them
  • Their customers need consumer-facing verification and brand assets — they route to us
  • Partner Program available: revenue share + white-label options for consultants

Ask: “Is your VP of Partnerships at NECANN? Let's get coffee after the show. We have a Partner Program I think you'd want to look at.”

4 · How to describe your role

Strategic advisor, not sales.

You're not BrandrAI's employee. You're their strategic advisor because you saw what they're building and thought it was real. That framing carries more weight at NECANN than a sales pitch — people are tired of being sold to.

Natural opener when someone asks:

“I was pharma counsel for years, and on the plaintiff side for a bit after. So I've seen what happens when document integrity goes wrong. When I saw what BrandrAI was doing on the cannabis side — cryptographic lab verification on-pack, Metrc anchoring, tamper-evident trail — I asked to advise them. I'm not selling anything. If you want an intro to Max, I'll make it.”

Your pharma-plaintiff background is an asset, not a detour. It positions you as someone who has seen downstream consequences — not a startup salesperson. Lean into it.
