1 · Program thesis

Fewer ambassadors, better ambassadors, longer commissions.

Most SaaS affiliate programs flood the market — 20-30% commissions for 12 months, open signup, low-effort referrers spamming discount codes on Reddit. BrandrAI's program is the opposite: 10% lifetime recurring, invite-only, 10-25 ambassadors year 1 max. The bet is that 5 highly-credible industry voices (Krysta Jones, THC Girls, 2-3 operator-turned-advisor types) will drive more pipeline than 500 random affiliate sign-ups — and they'll stick because the lifetime economics reward loyalty.

Who qualifies (the bar)
  • Real reach in regulated industries (cannabis, CBD, hemp, adjacent)
  • Documented audience: 10K+ engaged followers OR clear pipeline to operators
  • Reputation for being selective about what they promote
  • Max gets a gut check that they understand the product, not just the check
Who does NOT qualify
  • Anonymous Reddit / Discord affiliate farmers
  • "Promo code" YouTube review channels
  • Agencies reselling BrandrAI as part of a package (that's a Reseller tier — different program)
  • Anyone who won't sign the Ambassador Agreement

Program name / positioning: "BrandrAI Ambassadors" — not "affiliates" (which signals lower-tier). The word choice matters; ambassadors wear the badge in bio.

2 · Commission terms — exact mechanics

What "10% lifetime recurring" actually means.

TermMechanic
Commission rate10% of net recurring subscription revenue from referred customer
Definition of "net"Gross subscription revenue minus: refunds, chargebacks, Stripe processing fees, applicable taxes. NOT net of BrandrAI's own COGS.
What's includedPaid subscription tiers (Starter, Growth, Business, Enterprise). Monthly and annual plans both count.
What's excludedOne-time Studio credit pack purchases (non-recurring). Add-on services (trademark filing, custom dev). Free tier.
DurationLifetime of the referred customer's subscription. If customer churns and returns 12 months later via a different channel, commission does NOT reinstate automatically (first-touch attribution stops at 12-month inactivity).
AttributionLast-click within 60-day cookie window. Ambassador's referral link or promo code MUST appear in the signup flow for attribution.
Payment cadenceMonthly, on or before the 15th of the following month. Payments delayed 30 days from invoice date to allow refunds/chargebacks to clear.
Minimum payout$50 USD. Balances below $50 roll forward.
Payment methodACH (preferred), PayPal, or check. Ambassador provides payment info during onboarding.
Tax treatmentW-9 required before first payment if US. 1099-NEC issued annually if total > $600. Non-US: W-8BEN + self-certification.
ClawbackIf customer refunds or chargebacks within 60 days of signup, commission for that period is reversed. Commissions earned before that event are NOT clawed back.
Program terminationEither party can terminate with 30 days written notice. Commissions on customers referred prior to termination continue for the lifetime of their subscription — this is the "perpetual" part. New referrals stop accruing after termination.
Material breach exceptionIf ambassador breaches the Agreement (e.g. misrepresents BrandrAI, spam, fraud), BrandrAI can terminate immediately AND forfeit future commissions. Already-paid commissions are not clawed back.
Example math

Krysta refers 15 operators over 12 months. 8 sign up for Growth ($299/mo), 5 for Starter ($99/mo), 2 for Business ($799/mo). Stripe fees ~3%. Monthly commission: (8 × $299 + 5 × $99 + 2 × $799) × 0.97 × 0.10 = ~$450/mo recurring. Annualized: $5,400. If those customers stay 3 years avg, lifetime value of her referrals: ~$16,200.

Why 10% (not 20-30% industry standard): recurring + lifetime changes the math. A 20% one-year program tops out at 20% × $3,600 annual = $720. A 10% lifetime program at 3-year average tenure = 30% of customer LTV — same payout, better alignment (she's rewarded for retention, not just acquisition).

4 · Ambassador Agreement (template)

Template
BRANDRAI AMBASSADOR AGREEMENT

This Ambassador Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [DATE] between BrandrAI, Inc., a Delaware corporation with offices at [ADDRESS] ("BrandrAI"), and [AMBASSADOR FULL LEGAL NAME], an individual residing at [ADDRESS] ("Ambassador").

1. Appointment.

BrandrAI appoints Ambassador as a non-exclusive ambassador of the BrandrAI platform. Ambassador agrees to promote BrandrAI in good faith, accurately, and in a manner consistent with the public-facing marketing and product representations made by BrandrAI.

2. Commission.

2.1 Rate. BrandrAI will pay Ambassador a commission equal to ten percent (10%) of the Net Recurring Revenue (as defined below) received by BrandrAI from each Referred Customer, for as long as the Referred Customer maintains an active paid subscription to BrandrAI.

2.2 Net Recurring Revenue. "Net Recurring Revenue" means gross subscription revenue actually received by BrandrAI from a Referred Customer, less (a) refunds, (b) chargebacks, (c) payment processor fees directly attributable to the transaction, and (d) any sales, VAT, or similar transactional taxes. Net Recurring Revenue does NOT include one-time purchases (including Studio credit packs), professional services, trademark filing services, or any other non-subscription revenue.

2.3 Referred Customer. A "Referred Customer" is any BrandrAI customer who: (a) clicked Ambassador's unique referral link within 60 days before signup, or (b) entered Ambassador's unique promo code during signup, AND (c) was not an existing BrandrAI customer or active lead within BrandrAI's sales pipeline prior to the referral event. Attribution is last-click within the 60-day cookie window.

2.4 Payment. Commissions are calculated monthly and paid on or before the 15th day of the following month, via ACH (preferred), PayPal, or check. Payments are delayed 30 days from invoice date to allow for refund and chargeback clearance. Minimum payout is $50 USD; balances below $50 roll forward.

2.5 Clawback. If a Referred Customer's payment is refunded or reversed within 60 days of the payment date, the corresponding commission is reversed in the next payment cycle. Commissions already paid to Ambassador for prior periods are not clawed back.

3. Term and Termination.

3.1 Term. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated in accordance with this Section.

3.2 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other party.

3.3 Termination for Cause. BrandrAI may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Ambassador: (a) materially misrepresents BrandrAI's product, pricing, or terms; (b) engages in deceptive, spam, or otherwise prohibited marketing practices; (c) violates the Confidentiality provisions of this Agreement; (d) is convicted of a felony or crime involving moral turpitude; or (e) materially breaches any other provision of this Agreement.

3.4 Survival of Earned Commissions. Upon termination for convenience by either party, Ambassador shall continue to receive commissions on Referred Customers who signed up prior to the termination effective date, for as long as those Referred Customers maintain active paid subscriptions. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement. Upon termination for cause under Section 3.3, all future commissions (including on previously-referred customers) terminate immediately.

4. Ambassador's Obligations.

Ambassador agrees to:

  • Promote BrandrAI only in ways consistent with public-facing representations
  • Clearly disclose the commercial relationship when required by law (FTC endorsement guides, state consumer protection rules), including "#ad," "#partner," or equivalent disclosures on social media
  • Not spam, mass-email, or engage in black-hat marketing tactics
  • Not register domains containing "BrandrAI" or confusingly similar terms
  • Not bid on "BrandrAI" as a paid search keyword without BrandrAI's written consent
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations (including cannabis-industry regulations in any jurisdiction where Ambassador promotes BrandrAI)

5. BrandrAI's Obligations.

BrandrAI agrees to:

  • Provide Ambassador with a unique referral link and/or promo code
  • Provide Ambassador with access to a referral dashboard showing signups, conversions, and accrued commissions
  • Provide accurate and timely commission calculations and payments
  • Provide reasonable marketing materials (logos, product screenshots, talking points) that Ambassador may use in their promotion
  • Honor the surviving commission obligations in Section 3.4

6. Intellectual Property.

6.1 BrandrAI License to Ambassador. BrandrAI grants Ambassador a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use BrandrAI's trademarks, logos, and marketing materials solely for the purpose of promoting BrandrAI under this Agreement. Ambassador shall not modify BrandrAI's marks or use them in a manner that disparages BrandrAI.

6.2 Ambassador's Content. Ambassador retains all rights to content they create about BrandrAI (posts, videos, articles). Ambassador grants BrandrAI a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to share, repost, and repurpose Ambassador-created content about BrandrAI across BrandrAI's own channels, with attribution.

7. Confidentiality.

Each party may receive confidential information from the other party. Confidential information includes non-public business information, financials, customer lists, unreleased features, pricing strategies, and any information marked confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential. Each party agrees to hold the other party's confidential information in confidence for a period of three (3) years from disclosure (or longer if the information constitutes a trade secret), and to use such information solely for purposes of this Agreement. Exceptions: information that is publicly known, previously known by the receiving party, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law.

8. Independent Contractor.

5 · Referral tracking infrastructure

Two paths — build vs buy.

Path A: Buy (recommended for speed)
Rewardful (rewardful.com) — purpose-built for Stripe SaaS affiliate programs.
  • Starter plan: $49/mo (first 9% commission tier free)
  • Integrates with Stripe via webhook — commissions auto-calculated
  • Ambassador dashboard + payout automation
  • Cookie attribution + promo code tracking out of the box
  • Stands up in ~2 hours

Alternatives: FirstPromoter ($49/mo), PartnerStack ($$$), Tapfiliate. Rewardful has the cleanest Stripe integration and the simplest pricing for <50 ambassadors.

Path B: Build (defer to v2)
Custom attribution + commission engine in app.brandrai.com.
  • Ambassador model + referral_code + signup link generation
  • Cookie middleware to persist ref= param across signup
  • Commission calculation job running monthly
  • Payout integration (Stripe Connect / Wise / manual ACH)
  • ~2-3 weeks engineering

Revisit once program > 25 ambassadors or when Rewardful's pricing becomes material. Premature before then.

Recommendation: Path A (Rewardful). Stand it up next week, integrate with Stripe test mode, give Krysta her link for May 1. Total cost for year 1: $49-99/mo. Don't engineer an affiliate system when a $49/mo SaaS does it correctly.

8 · Risks, edge cases, guardrails

What can go wrong, and how to handle it.

Risk: Ambassador refers a customer who was already in your pipeline

Guardrail: Agreement Section 2.3 excludes "existing customer or active lead within BrandrAI's sales pipeline prior to referral event." Maintain a CRM log of leads with date stamps. Dispute resolution: Max reviews case-by-case.

Risk: Customer referred by Ambassador A, but signs up via Ambassador B's link later

Guardrail: last-click attribution within 60-day cookie window. Whoever's link was clicked most recently before signup gets the commission. This is standard and fair.

Risk: Commission liability on the books is too high for future fundraising

Guardrail: cap at 10-25 ambassadors year 1. Track total accrued lifetime commission as a SaaS-style liability metric. Model it in financial projections so any investor sees it upfront. Not a blocker at seed stage; could become a negotiation point at Series A+.

Risk: Ambassador goes rogue / misrepresents product

Guardrail: Agreement Section 3.3 allows immediate termination for cause, with forfeiture of future commissions. Document the breach with screenshots / recordings before terminating.

Risk: Ambassador program conflicts with Reseller program (separate tier)

Guardrail: mutual exclusivity — an entity cannot be both. Ambassador = individual or small brand with audience. Reseller = agency that sells BrandrAI as part of a package. Different margin structure, different agreements. Don't let the lines blur.

Risk: FTC disclosure violation

Guardrail: Agreement Section 4 requires "#ad" / "#partner" disclosure. Put it in the onboarding checklist. BrandrAI can be liable if ambassador fails to disclose and it's known to be a paid partnership.

Risk: Tax surprises for ambassador

Guardrail: W-9 / W-8BEN required before first payment. 1099-NEC issued annually if >$600 US. Onboarding explicitly mentions tax treatment. Ambassadors are responsible for their own taxes (Agreement Section 8).
